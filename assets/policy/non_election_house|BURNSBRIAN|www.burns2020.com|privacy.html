    PRIVACY POLICY

    Your privacy is critical to us. Brian Burns for Congress has created this Privacy Policy to disclose our privacy practices. This pertains to information collected while visiting our website, or www.Burns2020.com, mobile applications, and other online services. This Privacy Policy also applies to personal information collected in-person, by third-party vendors, by mail/e-mail or phone.

    • Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.

    • We will gather and utilization of individual data singularly with the target of satisfying those reasons indicated by us and for other good purposes, unless we get the assent of the individual concerned or as required by law.

    • We will just hold individual data the length of essential for the satisfaction of those reasons.

    • We will gather individual data by legal and reasonable means and, where fitting, with the information or assent of the individual concerned.

    • Personal information ought to be important to the reasons for which it is to be utilized, and, to the degree essential for those reasons, ought to be exact, finished, and updated.

    • We will protect individual data by security shields against misfortune or burglary, and also unapproved access, divulgence, duplicating, use or alteration.

    • We will promptly provide individuals with access to our policies and procedures for the administration of individual data.

    • We are focused on leading our campaign as per these standards with a specific end goal to guarantee that the privacy of individual data is secure and maintained.
