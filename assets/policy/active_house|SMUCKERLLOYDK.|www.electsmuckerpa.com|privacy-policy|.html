Privacy Policy

Your privacy is respected and we are committed to keeping you data secure. Your information will not be sold to any 3rd parties. The only time your information may be provided to outside parties is when we have contracted with them to provide a service such as a campaign mailing. We will take all reasonable steps to ensure that those parties do not disclose your information to others.

The information you choose to provide, including your name, e-mail, phone number and address will be used by our employees and volunteers to assist our campaign efforts. When we ask for personal information, we trust that your decision to provide it is voluntary, correct, and you are authorized to provide it. If you choose to provide your e-mail address, we may use it to send you updates on our campaign and remind you about important events and deadlines. You may unsubscribe from our e-mail list at any time. When you provide comments to us, whether by e-mail, blog, or otherwise, we assume that you do not intend these comments to be private, and we may, at our discretion, use your comments on our site or for general campaign purposes without asking for your permission.

Federal and state election laws require us to report information about donors to our campaign. We will not provide any information about you to federal or state government agencies unless we are required to do so by law.

We have taken all reasonable steps to make it safe for you to use your credit card and personal information to donate to our campaign. The software provider for these services uses advanced security technology to protect your data from theft or fraud. While we did not write the software, we have chosen only providers that we believe can best protect your privacy.

We may revise this policy from time to time. The policy you see here will reflect the latest changes. Please check back frequently if you want to make sure that you know how we are gathering, using, and protecting your information.

If you have further questions please contact us on our contact page.
