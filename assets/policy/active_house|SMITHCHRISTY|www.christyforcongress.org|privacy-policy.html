    Privacy Policy

     

    Christy Smith for Congress 2020 is committed to protecting your privacy online. While using our website, you do not have to identify yourself or divulge personal information. If you should choose to give us your personal information, you decide the amount of information you provide.

    Christy Smith for Congress 2020 is continually trying to expand online activism and to give voters the information they need promptly. As a result, we may periodically ask you to provide information such as your name, address, phone number and email address to enhance our ability to work with you as an online activist. If you choose not to give us any information, we may not be able to provide you with timely information regarding events and issues important to you as a politically interested and active citizen.

    Our site links to a limited number of other websites. Christy Smith for Congress 2020 is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of these sites.

    This privacy policy may be amended by Christy Smith for Congress 2020 at any time with no other notice than posting of such amendments on this site.

    How do we protect your information?

    Our website has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of the information under our control. When you contribute online, Christy Smith for Congress 2020 uses state-of-the-art security protocols to protect sensitive data, such as personal information and credit card numbers.

    Contacting us about privacy:

    If you have any questions about our privacy policy, the information we have collected from you online, the practices of this site or your interaction with this website, send an email to info@christyforcongress.org.
