Privacy Policy

The John Katko for Congress campaign is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience possible. This Statement of Privacy applies to this website and governs data collection and usage. By using this website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

 

Collection of your Personal Information

The John Katko for Congress campaign may collect personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address, and telephone number that you enter into the Contact Us form. The campaign may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests, and favorites.

 

Some information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by this or affiliated websites. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by the campaign for the operation of this site and to provide general statistics regarding use of the campaign website.

 

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through this website, this information may be collected and used by others.

 

The John Katko for Congress campaign encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from this website, so that you can understand how those sites collect, use, and share your information. The campaign is not responsible for the privacy statements or content on other Web sites.

 

Use of your Personal Information

The John Katko for Congress campaign collects and uses your personal information to operate the campaign Web site. The campaign may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of news and upcoming events. The campaign may also contact you to conduct research about your opinion on campaign related issues.

 

The John Katko for Congress campaign keeps track of the pages visited on the campaign website in order to determine which pages or entries are the most popular. This data is used to improve the overall usability of the website.

 

Use of Cookies

This website uses “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a webpage server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

 

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information that you have provided before. When you return to the same website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the features that you customized.

 

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the website during your visit.

 

Security of your Personal Information

The John Katko for Congress campaign makes every effort to secure your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

 

Changes to this Statement

The John Katko for Congress campaign may occasionally update this statement to reflect feedback. The campaign encourages you to periodically review this statement to be informed of how the campaign is protecting your personal information.

 

Contact Information

The John Katko for Congress campaign welcomes your comments regarding this statement. If you believe that the campaign has not adhered to this statement, please contact the campaign. We will use reasonable efforts to promptly determine and remedy the problem.
