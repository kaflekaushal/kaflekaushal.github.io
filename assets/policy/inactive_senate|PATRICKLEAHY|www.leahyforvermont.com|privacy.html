Privacy

Thank you for visiting my website and taking the time to review my privacy policy. Protecting individuals' right to privacy on the internet is extremely important to me and my privacy policy is simple: this website will collect no personal information about you unless you specifically choose to provide such information.

Some information about your visit to this site may be automatically recorded, but it will not identify you personally. Such information includes:

  • The internet domain and IP address from which you access this website;
  • The type of internet browser (for example, Netscape or Explorer) and the operating system used to access this site;
  • The time and date you visited this site;
  • The pages within this site that you visited.

When you sign up on my website, we may ask for your name, address, telephone number, email address, and other information. We use the information you provide to send you the latest news and updates from my campaign. We also work closely with Democratic candidates, parties and allied organizations and may share some basic contact information with them, and they may contact you.

If you have any specific questions or comments regarding this policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
