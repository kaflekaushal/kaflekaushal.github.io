 This Privacy Policy applies to the website www.maryfay4congress.com (the "website") owned and operated by Mary Fay 4 Congress, a congressional campaign.

Mary Fay 4 Congress has created this privacy policy to explain how we use information that you may provide while visiting this website and to demonstrate our commitment to Internet privacy.

Mary Fay 4 Congress may modify this policy from time to time so we encourage you to check this page when revisiting this website. By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

When you register, contribute, sign up to volunteer, or take any other action on our website, we may ask you to give us contact information, including your name, telephone number and/or e-mail address. We may obtain information about you from outside sources and add it to or combine it with the information we collect through this website. We use this information to operate this website, send you news and information about Mary Fay 4 Congress activities, solicit your participation in Mary Fay 4 Congress activities, and related uses. We use your e-mail address to send such information and may use your telephone number to call you or send you a text message or phone call for these purposes.

Additionally, we may share your information as follows, or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy: to companies that assist us in maintaining this website or with the Mary Fay4 Congress activities, but only for purposes of providing services to Mary Fay 4 Congress and with the strict requirement that any such company will never itself have the right to contact you for any reason. To comply with applicable law or legal requirements (for example, a lawful subpoena), to protect our rights or property, or to protect our supporters from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful conduct, or if we reasonably believe that an emergency involving immediate danger of death or serious physical injury to any person requires disclosure of communications or justifies disclosure of records. With candidates, organizations, committees, or groups that we believe share the same political or social viewpoints or objectives as Mary Fay 4 Congress; or to other groups with your consent.

Submitting your personal information (including e-mail address) on this page will sign you up to receive e-mails from Mary Fay 4 Congress.

CHILDREN

Mary Fay 4 Congress does not knowingly solicit and does not accept personally identifying information from children to third parties. Children should not use this website without adult supervision. If you have questions or concerns about your child’s access to the website or how the information is used, please contact us.

PROTECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION 

When you contribute through this website, we collect credit card information from you. That information is used solely for processing your contribution; is not maintained by Mary Fay 4 Congress; and is never disclosed to anyone, for any other purpose other than for processing your contribution, under any circumstances.

Mary Fay 4 Congress uses industry standard security measures to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of all of the personally identifiable information that you provide to us. Our server is located in a locked, secure environment. All supplied credit card information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our payment processing provider's database. 

Permission to access your personally identifiable information is granted only to you and Mary Fay 4 Congress employees or contractors who need to know that information to provide services to you and who are required to keep the information confidential. Although we make good faith efforts to store information collected by this website in a secure operating environment, we cannot guarantee complete security.

CONTRIBUTIONS TO MARY FAY 4 CONGRESS

Mary Fay 4 Congress is required to file regular reports with the Federal Election Commission that publicly disclose the name, address, occupation and employer of persons who contribute over $200 during an election cycle, along with the amount and date of the contribution. State and local election laws may also require us to disclose this information at a lower itemization threshold, depending on the committee's activity.

COOKIES AND DATA TRACKING

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Mary Fay 4 Congress website uses a cookie to compile aggregate data about visitors to the website and their interaction with the website for the purposes of improving the operation of the website and/or offering better user experiences and tools in the future along with measuring aggregate web statistics, including number of visitors, repeat visitors, popular pages and other information..

We may also use cookies to facilitate your online visit by maintaining data that you may provide when submitting forms so that you will not need to reenter certain information. If you wish to disable these cookies, the help portion of the tool bar on most browsers can assist.

We may also use third-party services such as Google analytics. This helps us understand traffic patterns and know if there are problems with our website. We may also use embedded images in emails to track open rates for our mailings, so that we can tell which mailings appeal most to Mary Fay 4 Congress supporters.

Please note that third party vendors, including Google, may show Mary Fay 4 Congress-sponsored ads on the Internet. Those third party vendors (including Google) use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Users may opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/.

HOW TO UNSUBSCRIBE or OPT OUT OF EMAILS

People who subscribe to email lists or fill out our forms may receive periodic updates from Mary Fay 4 Congress via mail, fax, e-mail, or text. You may opt out of receiving future information by using the unsubsubscribe procedure shown in our email.

ADVERTISING

We may place online advertising with third-party vendors, including Google, which will be shown on other websites. In some cases, those third-party vendors may decide which ads to show you based on your prior visits to the website. At no time will you be personally identified to those third-party vendors, nor will any of the information you share with us be shared with those third-party vendors. If you prefer to opt out of the use of these third-party cookies on the website, you can do so by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt out page .

Those third party vendors (including Google) use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. The privacy policies and practices contained in this Privacy Policy do not apply to ANY external links. This Privacy Policy only applies to our website and its subdomains. It does not cover websites that are linked to by this website for which we are not responsible ("linked-Sites"). We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of these linked-Sites. We encourage you to read the policies and practices of the linked-Sites and all sites you visit.

AMENDMENTS AND CONSENT TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We retain the right to amend or otherwise update this Privacy Policy at any time. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of the information as we have described. If we change our policies and practices, we will post the changes in our Privacy Policy so that you are always aware of them. With this knowledge, you can make an informed decision about whether you wish to provide personal information to us.
