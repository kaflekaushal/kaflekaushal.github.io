PRIVACY POLICY

Who We Are:

The Kuzmich for Congress campaign created this privacy policy to notify you how we use information that you may provide while visiting our website. Our website address is: http://www.kuzmichforcongress.com.

This privacy policy may need to be modified occasionally, so we encourage you to revisit this page when visiting our website.

Who We Share Your Data With:

When you register, make a contribution, sign up to volunteer, sign up for our newsletter, or take any other action on our site, we may ask you to give us contact information, including your name, address, telephone number, employer, profession, and/or e-mail address. We may obtain information about you from third parties and add it to or combine it with the information we collect through kuzmichforcongress.com. We use this information to operate this site, send news and other kinds of information to you about the Kuzmich for Congress campaign, request your participation in the Kuzmich for Congress campaign events and activities, and obtain and confirm RSVP’s to events and activities. We use your e-mail address to send such information by e-mail and may use your telephone number to call you for these purposes.

We will never provide your e-mail address or any of your personal information to any other person or organization, for any purpose. If you decide to make a contribution online, we may also ask for your credit card number and its expiration date. That information is used solely for processing your contribution through our payment gateway; is not maintained by our organization, and is never disclosed to anyone, for any other purpose other than for processing your contribution. We do have to comply with Federal law, which requires us to use our best efforts to collect and report the name, mailing address, occupation, and name of the employer of those individuals whose contributions exceed $200 in an election cycle.

Comments

When visitors leave comments on our site, we collect the data shown in the comments form as well as the user’s IP address and browser user agent string to help with spam detection.

An anonymized string created from your email address (also called a “hash”) may be provided to the Gravatar service to check if you are using it (the Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: https://automattic.com/privacy/). After approval of your comment, your profile picture will be visible to the public in the context of your comment.

Media

If you upload images to our website, you should avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS) included. If location data is embedded, visitors to our website can download and extract any location data from those images on the website.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user’s hard drive that contains information about the user or visitor.

The Kuzmich for Congress website uses a cookie for measuring our traffic and other analytics, including but not limited to the number of monthly visitors, number of repeat visitors, most popular pages, and other information. Kuzmich for Congress also uses cookies to provide functions that coincide with your online visit by maintaining data that you provide for these online activities. This is done so that you will not need to resubmit certain information.

We also use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Mailchimp. This helps us better understand our site traffic and e-newsletter performance. We may also use embedded images in emails to track open rates for our mailings, so that we can tell which mailings appeal most to our supporters.

If you leave a comment on our site, you may opt-in to saving your name, email address, and website in cookies. These are for your convenience so that you will not have to fill in your details more than once. These cookies will last for one year.

If you have an account and you log in to our site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for two days and screen options cookies last for a year. If you select “Remember Me”, your login will persist for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

Embedded Content From Other Websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as it would if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content, if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

How Long We Retain Your Data

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely. This is so we can recognize and approve any follow-up comments automatically instead of holding them in a moderation queue.

For users that register on our website (if any), we also store the personal information they provide in their user profile. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (however, usernames cannot be changed). Website administrators can also see and edit that information.

What Rights You Have Over Your Data

If you have an account on this site, or have left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where We Send Your Data

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service.

How We Protect Your Data

Kuzmich for Congress uses the industry standard in security to protect against the loss or misuse of the information under our control. Our server is located in a locked, secure environment. Permission to access your information is granted only to you and Kuzmich for Congress staff or contractors who need to know that information to provide our services to you. We make our best efforts to store the information collected through this website in a secure environment. However, we cannot guarantee complete security.

Other Disclosure of Your Information

We do our best to make every effort to maintain the privacy of our users, but we may need to disclose personal information when we have a good-faith belief that the release is appropriate to comply with the law (for example, a lawful subpoena), to comply with the FEC, to protect our rights or property, or to protect our donors, artists, and supporters from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful conduct, or if we reasonably believe that an emergency involving immediate danger of death or serious physical injury to any person requires disclosure of communications or justifies disclosure of records without delay.

How to Unsubscribe or Opt Out

People who subscribe to e-mail lists via kuzmichforcongress.com will receive updates from the Kuzmich for Congress e-newsletter via e-mail. If you provided your phone number or address, we may also contact you in that way. You may opt out of receiving future information via e-mail by using the unsubscribe procedure specified on the e-mail message. Doing so will remove you from our contact lists.

How to Contact Us:

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy can be emailed to the staff at the Kuzmich for Congress campaign atinfo@kuzmichforcongress.com.
