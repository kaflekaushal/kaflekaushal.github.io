Privacy Policy

Kurt Schrader for Congress takes  the private nature of your personal information very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat the information we collect when you visit our web site and/or register for updates.

Personal Information
Kurt Schrader for Congress will never sell, rent or share your personal information, including your e-mail address, with any third parties for marketing purposes without your express permission. Kurt Schrader for Congress may share your personal information for the purpose of delivering our e-mail to you or as required by law.

Usage Information
Kurt Schrader for Congress may also collect non-personal, aggregated information about use of our site. This information is not personally identifiable and will only be used to find out how people use our site. For example, this information will tell us how much time users spend on our site, from which other sites visitors came, and to what other sites visitors go. The collection of this information allows us to, among other things, prepare for traffic load demands.

Review and Access
Upon your request, we will provide you with a summary of the information we collect about you. You will have an opportunity to correct, update or modify this information.

Choice/Opt-Out
If we ever send you information by e-mail that you did not expressly request, we will provide you with an option by which you may request no future notices.

Tracking
Like most sites, Kurt Schrader for Congress makes use of browser “cookies.” Cookies are small text files placed on your computer’s hard disk by our server. Kurt Schrader for Congress will never use cookies to retrieve information from a computer that is unrelated to our site or services. Kurt Schrader for Congress may also use transparent GIF images. We use these images to track the e-mails that we send out.

Links to Other Sites
As part of the service, Kurt Schrader for Congress may create links allowing you to access third party sites. Kurt Schrader for Congress is not responsible for the content that appears on those sites and does not endorse these sites. Please consult those sites’ individual privacy policies in order to determine how they treat user information.

Security
All information described above is stored on restricted database servers.

Modification to this Policy
Kurt Schrader for Congress may change this policy, but we’ll post any changes here, so be sure to check back for future information.
