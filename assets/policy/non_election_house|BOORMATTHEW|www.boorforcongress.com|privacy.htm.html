Privacy Policy

I care about providing you with tools and information to manage and protect your online privacy. I have developed this privacy policy to inform you about what information this website collects, who may receive that information, what I may do with the information, and how long I keep it. While I have made every attempt to make this policy as clear as possible, if you have any further questions about it, feel free to contact me. See the links below.

I will not give out any information you submit at my site to any third parties except in compliance with a request of a law enforcement agency. Under no circumstances will your name, e-mail, phone number or address be sold to, or given to any other parties. I will never willfully sell, lease, or rent any of your or your personally identifiable information to any third party.

No personal information is collected through this website. This website currently has no applications or programs that gather information from your computer without your consent. There are no "Forms" to fill out and submit for contact. All contact is done through your own Apps, for example, your Email, your Facebook, your Twitter or your Snapchat applications. Even contributions are done on a different web site.

I tried to make this website safe and secure by providing information one way only - from me to you. All links will take you off my website and connect you directly to other websites. Be aware that those other websites may collect personal data. I suggest you check their privacy policy to see if and what information they collect and what they do with it.
