Privacy Policy

Welcome to the official Web site of Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress. Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress knows your right to privacy is important, especially online. This privacy policy has been created to answer any questions you may have regarding Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress’ data collection and protection policies.

 

What information is being collected at CathyforCongress.com?

The only personal information collected is information you give us. In an effort to grow our grassroots community online, Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress may ask you to submit information. This information will be used to provide you with information about online events, personal appearances, TV appearances, talking points, and other campaign-related information. You do not have to provide this information – we only ask that you do so that we can better communicate with you! Additionally, when you offer your personal information and/or choose to participate in our volunteer efforts, we use your information to personalize and customize Web pages and emails from Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress to you. We may also use the information to credit individuals for their actions in support of Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress. We’re thankful for your support, and we want to recognize you! Some information must be collected when you make a contribution. When you make contributions to Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress that aggregate to more than $200 in a single election cycle, federal law requires us to use our “best efforts” to collect your name, mailing address, employer, and occupation. Credit card information provided may be stored at the donor’s option. In the event, a donor exercises that option, all credit card information will be stored with Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress’ secure third party vendor, certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1, the most stringent level of certification. Any information shared is done at the donor’s own risk.

We ask that children under 18 do not submit their information. In accordance with the Federal Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA), CathyforCongress.com does not knowingly collect information from children. In accordance with the Federal Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA), Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress will never knowingly collect personal identifiable information from persons thirteen years old or younger.

 

How is the information collected at CathyforCongress.com used?

We will not sell your personal information. Under no circumstances will Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress sell your information to third parties or any commercial entities.

 

Is my information secure?

Protecting information you provide is important to us. Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress makes every effort to protect your information from the moment you enter your information to when it is stored on our secure servers. CathyforCongress.com uses a secure socket layer (SSL) – with the highest level of encryption commercially available – on pages where online visitors register or make a secure online donation using their credit card. Strict security measures are in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of any and all information pertaining to CathyforCongress.com. CathyforCongress.com uses a secured/encrypted server for online credit card donations. The online contribution form is in compliance with FEC regulations. Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress has put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to prevent unauthorized access.

CathyforCongress.com links to other sites: Our site may contain links to other websites and servers. Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress does not bear responsibility for privacy policies or content on linked websites. Because Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress has no control over the websites we link to, you agree and acknowledge that Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress is not responsible for the availability of resources or external sites and does not endorse and is not responsible or liable for any content provided therein. Accordingly, you also agree and acknowledge that Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress will not be responsible or liable, directly or indirectly, for any damage or loss caused or alleged to be caused by or in connection with use of or reliance of any such content, services on or through any such site or resource or goods. We encourage you to read the privacy statements on each website you visit. Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress provides links to original articles written and maintained by third parties. These links are provided as a convenience and do not imply a claim of ownership in that content.

 

How do I contact Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress?

If you have any questions, comments or concerns please contact Cathy McMorris Rodgers for Congress by e-mail using the contact information provided here.

 

Will this privacy policy change?

We have no intentions to make changes to this privacy policy, though we do reserve the right to revise or update this privacy policy at any time.

 

3rd Party Advertising Cookies Opt-Out

These vendors have their own privacy policies and may be members of the Network Advertising Initiative (“NAI”).  To remove yourself from some or all NAI member advertising programs, please visit the NAI Opt-Out Page and follow the relevant instructions.  Please note that if you delete, block, or otherwise restrict cookies, or if you use a different computer or Internet browser, you may need to renew your opt-out choice.

Notwithstanding anything else in this policy, we or a data provider we have engaged may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser to enable you to receive customized ads or content.   These cookies contain no personally identifiable information. The cookies may reflect de-identified demographic or other data linked to data you voluntarily have submitted to us, e.g., your email address, that we may share with a data provider solely in hashed, non-human readable form. To opt-out of these cookies, please go to https://www.aboutads.info/choices.
